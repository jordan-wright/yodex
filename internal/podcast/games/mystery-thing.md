# Mystery Thing

Goal: Guess the mystery thing using clues.

How it works:
- The host thinks of a mystery thing related to the topic of the day, without being too obvious.
- The host gives 4 clues, one at a time.
- Clues start broad and become more specific.
- After each clue, the listener is invited to guess.
- After the final clue, the host reveals the answer.
- All mystery things and clues must be kid appropriate.

Guidelines:
- Use descriptive, kid-friendly language.
- Avoid naming the answer until the reveal.
- End with a short fun fact about the mystery thing.
